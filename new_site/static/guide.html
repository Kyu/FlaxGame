Gameplay Guide

Welcome to This Yet to be named game! (Taking suggestions)

You're probably wondering what this is about.

Before I explain, here is some terminology.

Hex, Location: Each of those points on the map (1.1, 1.2, 1.3 etc)

Team: Named after a color. This is used to identify who controls where.

Turn: Every 10(currently at 5 for testing) minutes, a turn happens. In this turn, you get 1 action(max 10), your morale improves by 1( max 100), and for each hex, the ammo and population grows.

You'll need ammo to fight, and you can recruit 10 people from each hex, once the population is high enough. (This costs 1 action)

Fights go like this:

You attack a player, and based on your troop sizes and morale, a winner emerges. No fights are without loss. Attacking costs 1 action, defending costs none

How do I win?

You and your team members try to take control of the entire board, one location at a time. You can move from location to location by clicking on a neighboring location and clicking "Move here" (This costs 3 actions)

How do I take control of an hex?

Hexes have a set amount of points that can be captured, max 1000. At the end of every turn, the team with the most members in a hex gain positive points in that hex, while the others lose points if they had any.
